# PROJECT BRIEF

## Overview

AI MIDAS ACADEMY is a free public academy focused on deep written education about football prediction and football market intelligence. The academy teaches how to analyze matches, markets, risk, price behavior, probability, and structured research instead of selling signals or copy-betting shortcuts.

## Problem

Most football education products in this space collapse into one of four weak patterns:

- tip-selling disguised as education
- shallow betting content with no real structure
- hype-heavy claims about guaranteed profitability
- disconnected technical fragments with no learning system

AI MIDAS ACADEMY exists to replace that with a serious educational framework.

## Positioning

AI MIDAS is:

- a research-grade educational academy
- a system for structured football intelligence
- a bridge between football reading, market reading, and probabilistic reasoning
- a practical learning environment for research-minded operators

AI MIDAS is not:

- a tips brand
- a betting signal group
- a casual betting content site
- a guaranteed-profit promise machine

## V1 scope

- Static-first public academy website
- No login
- Deep written courses
- Framework pages
- Glossary
- Labs or practical exercise surfaces
- Scalable structure for future growth without backend dependence in V1

## Audience

Primary personas:

1. Trader Esportivo em Transicao
2. Analista Quantitativo Amador
3. Operador Cripto-Quant / Prediction Market Operator
4. Premium Report Consumer

## Core topics

- football prediction
- football research
- probability
- risk
- pre-live trading
- odds movement
- AI prediction systems
- prediction markets

## Product principles

- teach mechanisms, not magic
- prioritize clarity over entertainment
- make complex ideas visually understandable
- combine research rigor with practical usability
- stay accessible to serious self-directed learners

## Success criteria for V1

- clear academy identity from the first screen
- strong differentiation from betting hype brands
- reusable content structure for many courses
- static hosting compatibility
- design language strong enough to feel memorable and ownable
- documentation good enough to guide future Codex sessions without resetting decisions

## Non-goals for V1

- user accounts
- personalized dashboards
- subscription complexity
- community platform features
- live odds ingestion as a mandatory dependency
- building prediction engines before the academy system is established
