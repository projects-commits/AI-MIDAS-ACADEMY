# AI MIDAS ACADEMY

AI MIDAS ACADEMY is a free public academy for deep written courses about football prediction, football research, probability, risk, pre-live trading, odds movement, AI prediction systems, and prediction markets.

The project is being built as a static-first educational platform with a strong research identity. V1 has no login and is intended to be deployable on GitHub Pages or a simple VPS serving static output.

## Positioning

AI MIDAS is not a tips brand, not a betting signal group, not a casual betting site, and not a guaranteed-profit brand.

It is a research-grade educational academy that teaches people to transform football into structured intelligence: price, probability, risk, context, and research.

## V1 content directions

- Market Intelligence
- Probability & Modeling
- AI Prediction Systems
- Football Research
- Prediction Markets & On-chain
- Risk & Execution
