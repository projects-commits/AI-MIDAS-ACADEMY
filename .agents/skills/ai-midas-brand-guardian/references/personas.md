# AI MIDAS personas reference

## Priority personas

### Trader Esportivo em Transicao

- Wants to leave picks culture behind
- Needs method, context reading, and repeatable criteria

### Analista Quantitativo Amador

- Likes models and data
- Needs help translating theory into football decisions

### Operador Cripto-Quant

- Understands pricing and flow from digital markets
- Needs football context and market mapping

### Premium Report Consumer

- Consumes premium analysis
- Needs independent criteria to judge argument quality

## Messaging use

- Emphasize structure over excitement
- Emphasize process over confidence theater
- Emphasize decision quality over outcome bragging
