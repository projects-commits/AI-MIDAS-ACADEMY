# QA checklist

## Content

- Is the promise precise?
- Is the tone serious and practical?
- Are examples concrete?
- Are failure modes explicit?
- Is there a checklist or exercise where needed?
- Is the depth appropriate for the claimed scope?

## Product

- Do links work?
- Does the route structure make sense?
- Does the build pass?
- Is the page readable on mobile?
- Are headings, buttons, and panels distinguishable enough?

## Schema

- Do files validate?
- Are counts and links consistent?
